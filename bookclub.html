Bookclub

At YOUthversity, our Bookclub is more than just reading — it’s about connecting through stories, sharing thoughts, and discovering new perspectives together. Whether you’re into cozy standalones or epic sagas, there’s a place for you on our shelves.

Main Bookclub

We dive into a standalone book that fits a chosen theme — from self-discovery to adventure, from community to creativity, from timeless classics to recently published best sellers.

It’s a great way to explore diverse stories while reflecting on topics that matter to us. Everyone is valued and personally involved, from voting their preferred choice in a poll to expressing opinions in a spoken call or written discussion.

Parallel Bookclub

For the readers who love to get lost in longer tales, our Parallel Bookclub runs alongside the main one.

Here, we take our time — each book or saga spans longer, giving everyone space to enjoy the journey without rushing. Calls and discussion also follow a slower pace. Find out with us how interestingly writers' styles evolve through pages and volumes!



Bookish Actitivies

BookNook

Every two weeks, usually on Sundays, we meet in a cozy Discord call to read together for a few hours and each session includes a little reading prompt to make things even more engaging.

It’s a quiet moment to focus, unwind, and share the joy of reading side by side. Fill with us a community journal and enjoy a comforting body-doubling ambience provided by productivity apps/games.

Moreover, read-a-thon sessions may be hosted spontaneously!

Challenges

We love a good challenge! Our community hosts yearly and seasonal reading challenges — you can find them on Discord, GoodReads, and StoryGraph.

They’re a fun way to discover new genres, set personal goals, and celebrate your reading progress. We provide with less and more demanding levels of success, so no fear to be overwhelmed or to come across unreachable requests.

Library Hive

Like in a library, every member who signs up receives a customised virtual card where to collect and seal unique stamps.

Like in a hive, each contribution earns you a special reward. It’s our way of celebrating your journey as a reader within our buzzing community. Bees are a cooperative, hardworking and lovely, and we'll grant you with a sense of progression and self-fulfillment as you keep loving books at your own pace.

YOUthversity Bookshelf

Each month, we gather the books read by our members and add them to our community bookshelf.

It’s a growing visual collection that represents all the stories we’ve shared and the worlds we’ve explored together. Additionally, we'll discover the most popular languages and the overall satisfaction rate, thanks to our collective stats!



Where to find the bookclub and its activities

Discord

Our Discord server is the heart of the YOUthversity Bookclub — it’s where everything happens!

There you can find book announcements and reading schedules, books discussions and spontaneous chats.

It’s also where we host our Booknook sessions, Challenges, and the YOUthversity Hive.

Whether you’re sharing your thoughts on the current read or simply hanging out with fellow book lovers, Discord is the place to be.
