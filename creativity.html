Creativity

Creativity is about self-expression, connection, and exploration. Our creative spaces invite you to unwind, experiment, and share your passions — no expertise needed, just curiosity and good company.

Crafternoon

Get ready for a cozy burst of creativity! During Crafternoon, we come together to work on our favorite creative projects — whether that’s crocheting, painting, drawing, journaling, or any other craft that brings you joy. It’s a relaxed space to create, share, and enjoy the company of others while letting your imagination flow.

Cameras and microphones are completely optional — join however you feel most comfortable.

We meet every other Wednesday and Sunday, alternating each week. So grab your yarn, brushes, or sketchbook, and spend a calm and creative evening with us!

Puzzling

Our Puzzling Nights are all about teamwork, focus, and fun! We gather in a cozy Discord voice channel to solve jigsaw puzzles together using Tanggle, an online platform perfect for collaborative puzzling sessions.

Thanks to Tanggle’s generosity, our community enjoys access to the higher Pro tier — #GIFTED by the platform itself! That means more puzzles, more features, and even more opportunities to connect while we piece things together.

Join us, chat while puzzling, or simply relax and enjoy the shared sense of accomplishment as each image comes to life, one piece at a time.
