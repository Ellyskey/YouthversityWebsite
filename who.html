Who are we?

Behind YOUthversity is a small but dedicated managing team working together to keep the community welcoming, inclusive, and sustainable.

Each member brings their own skills, perspectives, and care into shaping our spaces, organizing activities, and supporting the people who join us every day.

We work collaboratively, listen closely to the community, and grow alongside it — because YOUthversity isn’t just something we manage, it’s something we’re part of
